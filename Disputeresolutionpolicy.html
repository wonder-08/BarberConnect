BarberConnect Dispute Resolution Policy

This Dispute Resolution Policy ("Policy") sets out the procedures for resolving disputes between users ("you") of the BarberConnect platform ("Platform") and between you and BarberConnect ("Company", "we", "us", or "our"). This Policy is incorporated by reference into our Terms of Service.

1. Notifying BarberConnect of a Dispute

If you have a dispute with another user of the Platform or with BarberConnect, you should report the dispute to us via our help center or by sending an email to support@barberconnect.com. Please include all relevant information, so we can understand and attempt to resolve the dispute.

3. Dispute Resolution Procedure

If we elect to help resolve a dispute, we will do so in good faith based solely on our interpretation of our policies. Our resolution of a dispute is final with respect to the Platform.

4. Escalation to Third-Party Mediation

If a dispute can't be resolved between users or between a user and BarberConnect directly, you agree that the dispute shall be referred to a neutral third-party mediator agreed upon by both parties. The costs of such mediation will be borne equally by the parties involved in the dispute.

5. Arbitration Agreement

If mediation does not resolve the dispute, you agree that any dispute or claim relating in any way to your use of the BarberConnect Platform will be resolved by binding arbitration, rather than in court, except that you may assert claims in small claims court if your claims qualify. The laws of South Africa apply.

6. Class Action Waiver

You may only resolve disputes with us on an individual basis and may not bring a claim as a plaintiff or a class member in a class, consolidated, or representative action.

If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the date at the top of the policy and, in some cases, we may provide you with additional notice.
